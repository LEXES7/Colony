Why Colony is the best way

Ownership

Your machine. Your data.

No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry. Code and conversations never leave your computer — there is nothing to leak.

Control

Nothing gets built without your approval. Requirements and architecture wait for your yes, and every task passes review.

Resilience

Nothing is ever lost.

Usage limit? Crash? Closed your laptop? One press of Resume and the team picks up exactly where it stopped.
