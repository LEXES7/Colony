Explore Colony.

A living office

Every project and team gets a room. Monitors glow while agents think, and messages travel between rooms as they collaborate — in 3D or crisp pixel art.

Real tasks, real ETAs

Work becomes estimated tasks with live countdowns, overdue flags and honest receipts — took 8 minutes against a 10-minute estimate.

Experts on every repo

Point Colony at your projects and each gets a resident expert. Ask how you solved something last year — get the answer with file citations.

Reviewed, tested, audited

Every task passes review. Testing traces each requirement through real code. A security audit turns findings into fix tasks automatically.

Recovers from anything

Hit a usage limit mid-build? Say "resume" and the team picks up exactly where it stopped. Nothing you approved is ever lost.

100% local

No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry. Your code and conversations never leave your machine. Free and open source under MIT.

Locked down by design.

Agents can't run commands, can't leave their folders, can't read secrets — and the whole platform binds to your machine only.
